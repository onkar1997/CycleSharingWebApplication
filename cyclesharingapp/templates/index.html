HOW IT WORKS ?

1.Borrow

Enter the bikes number into the app and tap " Start Ride ". You will get a code to open and close the lockbox.

2.Unlock

Use the key inside the lockbox to operate the U-lock that attaches the bike to its station.close the lockbox before you ride.

3.Ride

Have fun and stay safe!
Use the U-lock to keep the bike secure if you make stop along the way.

4.Return

When you are done,lock the bike back to a drop point and close the lockbox.Then go to the app and tap "End ride".

FAQ'S

UBike is a Bike/Cycle Sharing Web App, where user can book a cycle to travel to certain amout of destination and time.

UBike is very user-friendly and easy to use. You just need to open our site URL, register, login and enter select the from and to locations , make the payment and Go.

Payment is very easy, no Rocket Science! You can do payment via Debit Card.

UBike charges a fixed rate to unlock a vehicle and then charges extra Rs 5/- if you extend 1 hour of gap as you ride. Rides may also be subject to a minimum charge.

A dedicated Contct Us section is provided by us, in case if you have any queries or feedbacks for us. We try to get in touch within an hour.

After you have your account set up, you're ready to take your first ride. The UBike is specifically made for college campus, so you ride within the college campus.
